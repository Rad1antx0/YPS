Who we are

Build Solid Foundation

Well come to Yadav Public School come with us, we also teach children about the basic values of human beings such as honesty, kindness, generosity, courage, freedom, equality, and respect. At YPS, we believe in turning dreams into reality. Our institution is the ideal choice for aspiring engineers and doctors, providing a comprehensive and supportive environment for students aiming to excel in competitive exams such as JEE Mains & Advance, NEET [UG], KVPY, and SOF.
Dedicated and Dynamic Faculty
Our strength lies in our dedicated and dynamic team of experienced faculties who are committed to delivering high-quality education. These professionals bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise, ensuring that students receive the best guidance and support throughout their preparation journey.
Specialized Exam Preparation
YPS specializes in preparing students for some of the most challenging exams. We understand the unique requirements of each test and tailor our teaching methods to meet these needs, helping students build a solid foundation and master the subjects required for success in JEE Mains & Advance, NEET [UG], KVPY, and SOF exams.
Holistic Learning Environment
Our goal goes beyond helping students excel in exams. We aim to instill a burning passion for success, motivating them to reach their full potential. Through well-planned schemes, we provide top-notch faculties, comprehensive study materials, rigorous test series, and personalized guidance. We believe in nurturing students' talents and equipping them with the confidence and skills necessary to achieve their goals.
Comprehensive Resources and Support
At YPS, students have access to an array of resources that facilitate their learning and growth. Our meticulously curated study materials cover all aspects of the syllabus, ensuring thorough preparation. Regular test series simulate real exam conditions, helping students assess their knowledge, manage time effectively, and improve their performance.
Passionate and Driven Students
We foster an environment where students are encouraged to cultivate their passions and work towards their dreams with determination and confidence. Our supportive community helps them overcome challenges and stay focused on their path to success.
Commitment to Excellence
YPS is dedicated to excellence in education and is committed to helping students achieve their highest potential. Our focus is not only on academic success but also on building a strong foundation for future endeavors.

Join YPS today and embark on a journey of academic excellence, personal growth, and boundless opportunities. We are here to guide you every step of the way, helping you transform your aspirations into accomplishments and paving the way for a successful career in engineering or medicine.
