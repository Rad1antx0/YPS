Fee Due Date

Fees will be deposited in the months of April, June, October, and January, once for every quarter in the Punjab National Bank APS branch Jodhpur from the 1st to the 10th in each quarter from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Fees can also be deposited by local cheque in favor of Principal Army Public School Jodhpur and online as per the instructions given on the reverse page of the fee book.

Late fees: If the fee is not paid by the due date, late fees will be charged as follows:

  • From the 20th of the current month: Rs 100/-
  • Up to 1 month (in which the fee is due): Rs 200/-
  • Up to 2 months (second month after due month): Rs 400/-
  • Up to 3 months (third month after the due month): Rs 750/-

If the fee is being paid by cheque, it must be paid by the 9th of the due month. Late fees will be added with effect from the 10th onward.
